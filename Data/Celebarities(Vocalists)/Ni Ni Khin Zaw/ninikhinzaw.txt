Ni Ni Khin Zaw  is a Burmese pop singer. She won the 2008 season of Melody World, a televised singing competition. Ni Ni performed in the opening and closing ceremonies of the 2013 Southeast Asian Games in Naypyidaw.Ni Ni was born on 31 August 1991 in Yekyi, Myanmar. She attended and graduated from the University of Medicine 2, Yangon.
